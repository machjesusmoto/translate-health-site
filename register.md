# Get Started with Translate Health

**Turn everyday language into structured health data.**

---

Translate Health is an AI-powered health tracking service that turns your natural check-ins into structured data, surfaces patterns, and generates reports you can share with your healthcare providers.

## How It Works

1. **Provide your phone number** — This is how we'll communicate with you
2. **Consent to health data collection** — We collect only what you share, as described in our [Privacy Policy](/privacy)
3. **Consent to AI processing** — Your check-in text is parsed by AI to extract structured data (blood pressure, pain levels, sleep, etc.)
4. **Receive a one-time passcode via SMS** — Verify your phone number
5. **Start tracking** — Text us naturally, and we handle the rest

## Your Consent Matters

We require separate, explicit consent for data collection and for AI processing of your health data. Your consent can be withdrawn at any time through your [Settings](/settings) page or by texting **STOP** to unsubscribe.
