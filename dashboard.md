Your personal dashboard is where your health data comes together. Once available, you'll be able to review, correct, and explore everything you've tracked — all in one place.

## What You'll Find Here

### Daily Digest
Review and correct today's parsed check-ins. If the AI misread something ("7 out of 10" pain instead of "7 AM wake-up"), you fix it right here.

### Trends
Visualize blood pressure, pain levels, sleep quality, fatigue, and other tracked symptoms over time. See how things change week to week and month to month.

### Correlations
AI-detected patterns between your symptoms, sleep, triggers, and other factors. Things like "pain tends to spike two days after poor sleep" — patterns that are hard to spot on your own.

### Provider Reports
Generate structured, provider-ready reports for your doctor appointments. No more scrambling to remember what happened since your last visit. Share a clean summary with one tap.

### Data Export
Download your complete health history in standard formats. Your data is yours — you can take it with you anytime.
